Gluten Free bannana Bread Muffins

By: Delaney Brown

This recipe is completely gluten free and tastes amazing.

Supplies

  • Bowl
  • Mixer
  • Masher
  • Measuring cup
  • Teaspoons and Tablespoons

Ingrediants

  • 2 Ripe Bannanas(mashed)
  • 1/4 cup of Avacado Oil
  • 1/4 cup of Milk or applesuace
  • 1 tsp Gluten Free Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Gluten Free Flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips(optional)

Directions

Prep time= 25 minutes

Cook time= 30 minutes

Serving size= 12 servings

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.

Start with your 2 bananas and mash them till there is minimal clumps. Mix in the avacado oil and the milk untill completely mixed.

Then stir in the Vanilla extract, sugar and the eggs. Mix untill everything is completely combined

Next mix the gluten free flour, the baking soda and the salt. Mix untill completely smooth. Add in the

(optional)Add in the chocolate chips and stir.

Evenly destribute the muffins into a muffin pan. Make sure to not fill each completely full.

Put in oven for 30 minutes or untill baked fully throught. To check if its baked through use a toothpick and stick it in the middle, when you pull if out it should be mostly clean.
